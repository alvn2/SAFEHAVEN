## 1. Getting Started

### Creating Your Account

1. Go to [safehavenkenya.vercel.app](https://safehavenkenya.vercel.app)
2. Click **"Get Help Now"** or navigate to **Sign Up**
3. Choose a **pseudonym** (anonymous username — use anything you like)
4. Create a **passphrase** (your password — make it strong)
5. **IMPORTANT:** You will see a **12-word recovery phrase**. **Write it down on paper and keep it safe.** This is the ONLY way to recover your account if you forget your password.
6. You're in! No email, no phone number, no real name required.

### Logging In

1. Enter your pseudonym and passphrase
2. Your session will last for **15 minutes** of inactivity, then auto-lock for your safety

### Forgot Your Password?

1. On the login page, click **"Forgot Password"**
2. Enter your pseudonym
3. Enter your **12-word recovery phrase**
4. Set a new passphrase

> ⚠️ **If you lose your recovery phrase and forget your password, your account cannot be recovered.** This is by design — we cannot access your data.

## 3. Encrypted Journal

Your journal is **end-to-end encrypted**. Not even SafeHaven admins can read it.

### Writing an Entry

1. Click **"How are you feeling right now?"** or the **New Entry** button
2. Rate your **mood** (1-5), **energy** (1-5), and **sleep quality** (1-5)
3. Write your thoughts in the text area
4. Optionally add **tags** to categorize your entry
5. Click **Save**

### Audio Entries

1. Click the **microphone icon** in the journal editor
2. Record your voice note
3. The audio is encrypted and saved with your entry

### Viewing Past Entries

- Scroll through your journal to see past entries
- Your **mood chart** shows patterns over time
- Use the search to find specific entries

> 🔒 **Your journal entries are encrypted with your passphrase before leaving your browser. The server only stores encrypted text that nobody else can read.**

## 4. Safety Plan

Your safety plan helps you during moments of crisis.

### Creating Your Plan

Go to the **Safety Plan** tab and fill in:

1. **Warning Signs** — How do you know you're starting to feel bad?
2. **Coping Strategies** — What has helped you calm down before?
3. **Safe Contacts** — People you trust (friends, family)
4. **Professional Contacts** — Therapists, counselors, hotlines
5. **Environment Changes** — Things you can change about your surroundings

> 📱 **Your safety plan is available offline** — you can access it even without internet.

## 5. Peer Support Forum

The forum is a safe space to share and connect anonymously.

### Posting

1. Go to **Forum** from the navigation
2. Click **"Share Your Thoughts"**
3. Choose a **category** (Anxiety, Depression, Relationships, Grief, Self-Care, General)
4. Write your post with a title and body
5. Check **"Contains triggering content"** if applicable — this blurs the post for other users
6. Click **Post** — you'll appear with an anonymous name

### Interacting

- **Hug** 🤗 — Send support without words (click the heart/hug icon)
- **Reply** — Click reply to start a threaded conversation
- **Flag** 🚩 — Report harmful content for moderator review

> 🔵 **Triggering content is automatically blurred.** Users can choose to reveal it.

## 6. Finding a Volunteer

### Browse Volunteers

1. Go to **Volunteers** from the navigation
2. Browse the list of verified volunteers
3. Use the **search bar** to find by name, specialization, or language
4. Filter by **role** (Licensed Professional, Intern, Peer Listener)

### Volunteer Profiles Show

- Name and photo
- Qualifications and specializations
- Languages spoken
- Location
- Online/offline status
- Verification badge (✅ means admin-verified)

### Becoming a Volunteer

1. Click **"Become a Volunteer"**
2. Fill in the application form (name, email, qualifications, experience)
3. Submit for admin review
4. Once approved, you'll get access to the Volunteer Dashboard

## 9. Security & Privacy

### What SafeHaven NEVER Collects

- ❌ Real name
- ❌ Email address
- ❌ Phone number
- ❌ Location data
- ❌ IP address logs

### What SafeHaven Encrypts

- ✅ Journal entries (end-to-end encrypted)
- ✅ Safety plan data
- ✅ Chat messages
- ✅ Your password (bcrypt hashed — we can't see it)

### Quick Exit

If you need to quickly leave SafeHaven:
- Click the **Quick Exit** button (always visible in the navigation)
- Your browser will immediately redirect to a safe website (Google)

### Deleting Your Account

1. Go to **Security Center**
2. Click **"Delete All My Data"**
3. Confirm — this **permanently** erases everything and cannot be undone
