### 3.2 Vesting Schedule

All founder equity is subject to a **4-year vesting schedule** with a **1-year cliff**:

| Period | Vested |
|--------|--------|
| 0–12 months | 0% (cliff period) |
| 12 months | 25% vests at cliff |
| 13–48 months | Remaining 75% vests monthly (1/36 per month) |

**Acceleration:** If the Company is acquired (Change of Control), all unvested shares of active founders shall **immediately vest in full** (single-trigger acceleration).

### 3.3 Departure Scenarios

| Scenario | Unvested Shares | Vested Shares |
|----------|----------------|---------------|
| **Voluntary departure (Good Leaver)** | Returned to company pool | Founder retains |
| **Termination for cause (Bad Leaver)** | Returned to company pool | Company has 90-day call option at fair market value |
| **Death / disability** | Immediate full acceleration | Transferred to estate |

## 8. Dispute Resolution

### 8.1 Good Faith Negotiation
Founders agree to first attempt to resolve disputes through good-faith negotiation between themselves.

### 8.2 Mediation
If negotiation fails, disputes shall be submitted to mediation under the rules of the **Nairobi Centre for International Arbitration (NCIA)**.

### 8.3 Arbitration
If mediation fails, disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration under the NCIA rules. The arbitration shall take place in Nairobi, Kenya.

### 8.4 Deadlock Resolution
In case of a deadlock on a critical business decision:
1. Both founders present their position in writing
2. An independent advisor (mutually agreed) provides a recommendation
3. If still deadlocked after 30 days, either founder may initiate a buy-sell provision (shotgun clause)
