## Slide 2: The Problem

- **56 million** Kenyans. **< 500** psychiatrists. Ratio: **1 : 112,000**.
- **75%** of those needing help never receive it.
- **60%** fear social stigma associated with seeking mental health care.
- Youth suicide rates in Kenya are among the highest in East Africa.
- A single therapy session costs **KES 3,000–8,000** — unaffordable for most.

> *"Mental health is the invisible pandemic in Kenya."*

---

## Slide 3: The Solution

**SafeHaven** is a free, anonymous web platform that connects people in distress with trained volunteers — no email, no phone number, no real name required.

**Core offerings:**
- 🔒 End-to-end encrypted personal journal with mood tracking
- 💬 Anonymous peer support forum with trigger-content protection
- 🩺 1-on-1 secure chat with verified counselors and peer listeners
- 📋 Personal safety plan builder for crisis management
- 🤝 Community groups and mental health events directory
- 📚 Curated resource library (articles, books, videos)
